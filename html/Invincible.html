The All-New Amazing: INVINCIBLE














"Invincible" follows the story of a teenage superhero named Mark Grayson, who is the son of the world's most powerful superhero, Omni-Man.
Mark develops his own superpowers and takes on the mantle of Invincible,
fighting evil alongside his father and other heroes in their world. As the series progresses, Mark must face various challenges,
both personal and global, as he navigates his dual identities as a superhero and a regular teenager.

The series is known for its graphic violence and mature themes,
as well as its exploration of the superhero genre and its tropes.
