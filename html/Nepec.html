Jew-Jitsu Kaisen Part:85














"One Piece" is a long-running manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda.
Volume 96 of the series was released in Japan on September 4, 2020, and contains chapters 965 through 974.

Volume 96 features intense action sequences and epic battles,
as well as a deeper exploration of the characters' motivations and backstories.
It also reveals some key plot points that set the stage for the upcoming arcs in the series.
