The Fantastic Four














"Fantastic Four" is a superhero comic book series published by Marvel Comics.
It was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby and first debuted in 1961.
The series follows the adventures of a team of superheroes who gain their powers after being exposed to cosmic radiation during a space mission.

The team is made up of four members: Reed Richards (Mr. Fantastic), Susan Storm (Invisible Woman), Johnny Storm (Human Torch), and Ben Grimm (The Thing).
Each member has unique abilities: Reed can stretch and contort his body, Susan can become invisible and create force fields,
Johnny can control fire and fly, and Ben possesses superhuman strength and durability.
