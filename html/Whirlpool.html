Whirlpool









"Whirlpool" is a thriller novel by British author Colin Forbes, published in 1991. The book tells the story of a journalist named Mark Treasure, who investigates a series of bizarre and seemingly unrelated incidents, including a plane crash and the murder of a Russian diplomat in London.

As Treasure delves deeper into the case, he discovers a plot by a shadowy organization known as the Brotherhood to destabilize the world's governments and establish a new world order. With the help of a female Russian spy and an American journalist, Treasure races against time to uncover the truth behind the Brotherhood's sinister plans.
