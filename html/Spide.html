The Amazing Spiderman: #800














In this issue, Spider-Man faces off against his arch-nemesis, the Green Goblin, in a climactic battle that takes place in New York City.
The Green Goblin has been targeting the people closest to Spider-Man, including his loved ones and his fellow heroes,
in an attempt to break him down and defeat him once and for all.

The issue is written by Dan Slott, who had been the series' writer for several years,
It also includes a number of variant covers by different artists.

Volume 800 of "Amazing Spider-Man" is a significant milestone in the series' history and marks a major turning point for the character.
It features an epic battle that tests Spider-Man's abilities and his relationships with the people in his life,
and sets the stage for future storylines in the series.
