Strange Case of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde

Dr. Jekyll develops a potion that allows him to transform into a completely different person - the violent and amoral Mr. Hyde. Initially, Jekyll is able to control his transformations and enjoys the freedom that the release of his dark side brings. However, as time goes on, Jekyll finds himself losing control over his transformations and becomes increasingly dependent on his alter-ego, Mr. Hyde.

As the story progresses, Jekyll's friends and colleagues become increasingly alarmed at his strange behavior, and they start to uncover the truth about his connection to Mr. Hyde. The novella builds towards a dramatic and tragic climax, as the two sides of Jekyll's personality come into conflict and threaten to destroy him.
